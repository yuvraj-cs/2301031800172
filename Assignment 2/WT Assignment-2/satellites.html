Satellites

Satellites are objects that orbit around planets. They can be natural, like moons, or artificial, like the International Space Station. They serve various purposes from communications to weather monitoring.

Learning About

The Moon

Earth's only natural satellite is the Moon, which is about a quarter of Earth's diameter and is the largest natural satellite in the Solar System relative to the size of its planet.

Learning About

ISS

The International Space Station is a modular space station in low Earth orbit. It's a multinational collaborative project involving NASA, Roscosmos, JAXA, ESA, and CSA.
